Overview

Martellos Manor is a private residence owned by an individual known only as "Martellos." The location gained notoriety following the horrific events that transpired on June 19, 2025, at 21:47 (9:47 PM). The manor has since become the subject of intense speculation and investigation within online communities.

The property appears to be a secluded estate, though its exact location remains unknown to the public. What is known comes primarily from chat logs and testimonies from individuals who claim to have been present during the incident.

The June 19th Incident

On June 19, 2025, at approximately 21:47, a series of disturbing events occurred at Martellos Manor that would later be referred to as "The Horrors." The incident involved multiple individuals, including someone referred to as "Fipters" and "the bald man."

"The Horrors from 19th of june 21:47 2025 will never be forgotten."
— Manor description

According to recovered chat logs, the situation escalated quickly, with references to weapons, threats, and a general atmosphere of terror. The exact nature of what transpired remains unclear, but witness accounts suggest it involved:

  • Multiple individuals in conflict
  • References to weapons and violence
  • A mysterious "bald man" who appeared to know the location
  • Attempts to fix or address some kind of equipment malfunction

Key Figures

Martellos

The owner of the manor, Martellos appears to be the central figure in the incident. Chat logs suggest he was dealing with some kind of equipment malfunction and was increasingly agitated as events unfolded.

Fipters

A visitor to the manor who engaged in conversation with Martellos. Their presence during the incident suggests they may have been a witness to the events.

The Bald Man

A mysterious figure mentioned in the chat logs who apparently "knows" the area and was somehow connected to the escalating situation.

The Goon Room

A mysterious figure associated with the secret chair room, often referenced in connection with the "goon corner." The exact nature of this entity or individual remains unclear, though their presence seems to be a source of unease among manor visitors.

Manor Layout & Architecture

Recent reconnaissance has revealed disturbing details about the manor's construction and room arrangements. The building appears to have been designed with specific purposes in mind, though the architect's identity remains unknown.

The Roof Situation

Aerial observations have confirmed that the manor's roof contains no traditional rooms or living spaces. This unusual architectural choice has raised questions about the building's intended use and structural integrity.

The Secret Chair Room

Perhaps most disturbing is the discovery of what witnesses have termed the "goon room" - a secret chamber containing nothing but a single chair positioned in the center. The room appears to be accessed through hidden passages and serves an unknown purpose.

⚠️ Investigation Alert

The purpose of the isolated chair room remains unclear. Witnesses report an unsettling atmosphere and have nicknamed it the "goon corner." The room's sparse furnishing and secretive location suggest it may serve a specific, undisclosed function.

Chat logs reference this space with phrases like "goon corner" and "IT IS THAT ROOM MAM," indicating both familiarity with and unease about the location among those who have encountered it.

Current Status

The manor remains active according to recent reports, though its exact location continues to be unknown. The events of June 19th have entered internet folklore, with the phrase "The Horrors from 19th of june 21:47 2025 will never be forgotten" becoming associated with the incident.

Recent developments suggest ongoing activity at the manor, with Martellos continuing to invite visitors and discuss architectural modifications. The discovery of the secret chair room adds a new layer of mystery to the already enigmatic location.

No official investigation has been confirmed, and the true nature of what occurred at Martellos Manor remains a mystery.
